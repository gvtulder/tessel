Tessel is a colorful tile placement game. Place tiles with matching colors to make shapes and fill the board. The larger the shape, the higher the points.

Features:
<ul><li>Play an easy square game, or choose a more challening pattern.</li><li>Customize your game settings to change the rules.</li><li>Create your own tiling patterns in the drawing tool.</li></ul>

Choose from a wide range of tiling patterns to form endlessly varied tesselations:
<ul><li>Square</li><li>Triangular</li><li>Rhombille</li><li>Cairo pentagonal</li><li>Hexagonal</li><li>Deltoidal-trihexagonal</li><li>Penrose P3</li><li>Snub square</li><li>Ammann-Beenker</li></ul>
