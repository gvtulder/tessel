How to play?

  • Drag a tile from the stack to place it on the board. Click on a tile to rotate it.
  • Match the colors with the tiles next to it.
  • Complete a closed shape of one color to earn points. The bigger the shape, the higher the points.

The game ends when you have placed all your tiles.

Settings

  • Language
  • Light mode, dark mode,
    or follow your device
  • Show placeholder tiles
  • Highlight valid positions
  • Auto-rotate tiles
  • Snap tiles into place

Up for a challenge?

Played the triangles and squares? Try some of the other shapes and tiling patterns in a custom game.

Want to mix things up? Change the rules; require that colors should not match, or use only tiles with unique colors.

Note: some combinations may make it impossible to make a valid tiling! The game will warn you if that happens.
