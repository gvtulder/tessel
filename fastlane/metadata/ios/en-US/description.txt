Tessel is a colorful tile placement game. Place tiles with matching colors to make shapes and fill the board. The larger the shape, the higher the points.

Features:
* Play an easy square game, or choose a more challenging pattern.
* Customize your game settings to change the rules.
* Create your own tiling patterns in the drawing tool.

Choose from a wide range of tiling patterns to form endlessly varied tesselations:
* Square
* Triangular
* Rhombille
* Cairo pentagonal
* Hexagonal
* Deltoidal-trihexagonal
* Penrose P3
* Snub square
* Ammann-Beenker
